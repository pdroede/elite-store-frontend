Your personal information is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized personnel. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Payment information is processed by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@elitestore.com
